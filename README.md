Each stakeholder has a distinct focus:

CEO: Overall business health and strategic investment.
Regional Managers: Performance within their territory.
Sales & Customer Success: Customer value and retention.
Merchandising: Product portfolio optimization.
Marketing: Growth opportunities through advertising.




## Executive Leadership (CEO)
Goal: Understand overall business performance and where growth opportunities exist.

Business Questions
What are our current sales, profit, orders, and profit margin?
How has business performance changed over time?
How do sales, profit, and orders compare across regions?
Which region and product category combinations generate the highest profits and the biggest losses?
Which states are driving growth, and which require strategic attention?
Which states represent the greatest opportunities for future investment?
